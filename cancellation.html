Cancellation Policy & Refund
  • Canstruct connects consumers and service providers of all types (individually, a "SP"). We help consumers discover and select a SP based on characteristics that matter most to them.
  • In the unlikely event that customer decides to cancel the project after paying the booking amount which is 5% of project value, Canstruct offers a refund of 2% of the project value.
  • Refund amount will be transferred to the bank account provided by the customer.
  • If any design is sent to the customer or if any work is initiated by the professional at the customer's site, Canstruct will be unable to provide any refund
